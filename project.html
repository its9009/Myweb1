Cakes Heaven

"CakesHeaven" is a delightful website created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's like a virtual bakery filled with delicious cakes and treats! You can explore a wide variety of cakes, from classic flavors to unique creations.users can also customize their cake orders. Each cake comes with a description and beautiful pictures to make your mouth water. Whether you're a cake connoisseur or just love sweets, "CakesHeaven" is the perfect place to satisfy your cravings and discover new favorites.
